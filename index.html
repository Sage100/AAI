Anabaptist Aid Initiative (AAI)

AAI administers the SALT program in Nigeria in cooperation with Brethren Hope for the Nations (BHN) and Christian Aid Ministries (CAM). This site exists to clearly identify our registered organization for official and public reference.

Who we are

Mission

To serve Nigerian communities through Christ‑centered teaching and practical programs that foster savings discipline, vocational skills, and long‑term well‑being.

What we do

We coordinate and support SALT Savings groups and Living Hope Vocational School (LHVS) programming in cooperation with our partners.

Relationship to SALT, BHN, and CAM

AAI is the registered Nigerian NGO that administers the SALT program locally. We work in cooperation with Brethren Hope for the Nations (BHN) and Christian Aid Ministries (CAM) as part of the broader ministry framework in Nigeria.

Programs we support

Through the SALT program we facilitate savings groups, youth engagement, and vocational training via LHVS. These initiatives cultivate disciplined saving, quality teaching, and measurable growth.
