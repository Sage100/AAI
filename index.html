Relationship to SALT, BHN, and CAM

AAI is the registered Nigerian NGO that administers the SALT program locally. We work in cooperation with Brethren Hope for the Nations (BHN) and Christian Aid Ministries (CAM) as part of the broader ministry framework in Nigeria.

Programs we support

Through the SALT program we facilitate savings groups, youth engagement, and vocational training via Living Hope Vocational School. These initiatives cultivate disciplined saving, quality teaching, and measurable growth.
